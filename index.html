About Us

Getafix Solutions is providing unmatched customer experience & delight in construction & construction services. A trendsetter in implementing systems and processes for project management & monitoring service delivery with specific focus on timely and within budget delivery, Getafix is winning hearts and setting trends in the construction sector.

Unmatched customer delight and user experience through timely and quality deliveries is key to Getafix Solutions’ service policy and is well placed to be your trusted partner in catering to all your Construction and Construction Services needs, be it a new development, redevelopment, or Construction Services like remodeling and repair jobs in residential, institutional or commercial projects.

A start-up backed by legacy organizations which have been building landmark projects for 30 years, Getafix Solutions is gaining Customers’ trust by leveraging the latest technology for project management and monitoring; led by an experienced team of professionals and technocrats; adopting our time-tested methodology of partner and alliance selection and sustained by the organizational DNA of fairness, honesty, transparency and integrity in every sphere of activity.

Our Design Thinking has evolved a project monitoring and management framework that tracks all aspects of our projects and services throughout the lifecycle making us your trusted GO-TO Service Provider. Our experienced team of experts will be happy to schedule a visit as per your convenience, assess the scope of work, evaluate various options, as applicable, through an interactive process, prepare a work calendar and budget and finalize the project after discussions with you.
